About

The Photographer

Hi, I'm Kshithi — a high school photographer based in Denver, Colorado. I've been drawn to photography for as long as I can remember, finding stories in everyday light and overlooked moments.

My work spans portraiture, landscape, and architecture, always with an eye toward the emotional weight a single frame can carry. Photography has taught me to slow down, observe, and look twice.
